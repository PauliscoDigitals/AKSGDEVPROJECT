Akwa Ibom State Trending News

Flag off of Ibom Sport Festival

Governor Umo Eno flags off the ibom Sport Festival 2025 at the uyo township stadium on Monday 3rd September, 2025.

Governor Approves Sports Academy in Eket

Governor Umo Eno, during the opening of the Akwa Ibom Sports Festival 2025, approved the establishment of a sports academy in Eket LGA. The government has begun purchasing world-class sporting equipment, and is also developing modern facilities such as a nine-hole golf course and indoor/outdoor games center at the upcoming ARISE Resort Park, set to open in December 2025

Firms Join Forces to Drive Entrepreneurship

A landmark collaboration has been formed between the Davidson Foundation, Akwa Ibom State Association in the Netherlands (AKISAN), and Start Innovation Hub (SIH). The partnership, recently signed in Uyo, aims to empower Akwa Ibom’s youth with digital skills, focusing on machine learning, entrepreneurship, and creative innovation—all to bolster participation in the digital economy.
